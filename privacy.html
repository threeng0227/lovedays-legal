Couple Companion

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1
Introduction

Love Days ("Couple Companion") is a fully offline couple app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you interact with while using our app.

2
Information We Collect

We do not collect or transmit personal information to our servers. All your couple data — names, photos, memories, plans, and relationship dates — is stored only on your device using local storage (Hive).

3
Local Storage Usage

Your data is stored locally on your device using secure embedded storage. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data. We recommend backing up important memories externally.

4
Analytics Usage

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics — such as which features are used most. No personal or identifiable information is ever sent. Analytics collection is disabled in development builds.

5
Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings. Premium themes can be unlocked by watching rewarded ads.

6
Notifications

If you enable notifications, we schedule local reminders for your plans and anniversaries. Notification data never leaves your device — we do not use push notification services.

7
Data Protection

Since all data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's own security settings. We recommend enabling screen lock and device encryption for maximum protection.

8
Third-party Services

Our app integrates the following third-party services: Google AdMob (advertising), Firebase Analytics (anonymous usage analytics), Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting). Each service has its own privacy policy.
